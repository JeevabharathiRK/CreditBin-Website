About Us

Creditbin, created by Jeevabharati R, Sasidharan A, Boomika M, and Hemamalini S, guided by Maragatharaj S, aims to transform waste management. Its unique incentive system, based on credit points, promotes sustainable practices. Recognized for its effectiveness, Creditbin won the Quest Ingenium contest and secured an internship with Dr. T.C. Ramesh. With meticulous detail and collaborative effort, Creditbin offers a robust solution to waste management, empowering users to reduce waste and earn rewards. Positioned for global impact, Creditbin underscores the power of innovation and collaboration in addressing environmental challenges under Dr. T.C. Ramesh's mentorship.

Join us in our mission to create a cleaner, greener future for generations to come!
